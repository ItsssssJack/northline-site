// FAQ

Common questions.

How long does a typical project take?+
Launchpad runs four weeks. A standard Studio engagement is six to eight weeks from kickoff to live deploy, and we publish a public Linear board so you can see exactly where every milestone sits.
Do you work with companies outside the UK?+
Yes. Roughly 60% of our clients are in the US and EU. We schedule one short overlap window each day and run async on Loom, Figma and Linear for everything else.
Can we keep editing the site after launch?+
Absolutely — that's the whole point. We hand over a CMS your marketing team can actually use, plus a 60-minute editor training session. Most Studio clients ship a new page within their first week.
What stack do you build on?+
For most marketing sites: Next.js, Tailwind, and a headless CMS (Sanity or Contentful), deployed on Vercel. Where speed-to-edit matters more than custom interactions, we build in Webflow.
Will we work directly with the people building the site?+
Always. There are no account managers at Northline. You get a dedicated designer, a dedicated engineer, and a project lead — and you talk to them in a shared Slack channel from day one.
What if we already have a brand we like?+
Even better. Roughly half of our projects start from an existing identity. We'll audit it for screen and code, fix the gaps, and turn it into a working design system.
